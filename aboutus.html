About Us

Founded by 2 best friends with a shared passion for tea, flavor, and a good dose of fun. We believe that life is meant to be savored, and every sip should be packed with joy and a pop of personality. Our mission is to blend the freshest teas, creamiest milk, and most exciting flavors to create the perfect bubble tea. From classic milk tea to our bold fusion flavors, each cup is crafted with love, laughter, and a sprinkle of creativity. Whether you’re a seasoned tea lover or trying boba for the first time, we have something just for you. We pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients, ethically sourced teas, and hand-picked toppings. Our team of baristas are boba artists, here to help you find your new favorite flavor or customize your own creation. We’re more than a store—we’re a community of bubble tea enthusiasts brought together by a shared love of flavor, fun, and fantastic drinks. So, grab a friend (or make a new one here!), and let’s celebrate the little moments—one bubble at a time.
